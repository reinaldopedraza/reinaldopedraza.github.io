Biography

    I am part of the future generation of Computer Engineers, Digital Designers, and a future business owner. Over the years I have grown attached to digital media, whether that’s designing my own logo all the way to the hardware that makes this digital world a possibility. My future goal is to run a successful business that provides the best computer builds for my customer’s needs. Ever since a young age I've been interested in what parts go into make a computer, and the process that goes into make digital designs. Whether that’s a logo for my future company or a poster for a show we are hosting in our school. With programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, etc. you are given the ability to influence what a person sees and feels when they look at your work. Over the years I've developed my artistic style which consist of pastel colors and typography. Even though i really enjoy using geometrical shapes in my design. I've fallen in love with these programs that i have gone as far as getting certified in the different programs. As of 2016 I have certified in Adobe Illustrator and plan on getting certified in more Adobe products. Not only that I've taken it upon myself to learn about the hardware that goes into the computers we use on a daily basis. At the end of the day I’m constantly trying to improve on the skills I already have, and learn more about the hardware behind it all.

Goals

Clothing Company

I've always wanted to run a successful clothing company due to the fact that the clothes we wear makes such a big impact on our personalities. And if I am able to make my own clothing line not will I be able to show my personality through it but other people can as well.

Digital Designer

I've always dreamed of being a digital designer. I would always be amazed by the pieces designers would make for movies and company logos. This is one of the reasons why I have been inspired to work in Illustrator and Photoshop.

Computer Engineer

I've always been intrested in computers. I've always wanted to know of the components that make the computers we all use. I researched parts and how to build computers and I have gone as far as building my own computer. I want to better my knowledge on computers and pursue a career in computer engineering.

Computer Engineering Business

As I stated before I love building computers and have always been intrested in computers and the parts that go into them. So if i was able to run my own business and build computers for a persons specific needs and make the customer satisfied that would mean i accomplised my goal.
